Cristiano Ronaldo

About Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players in the history of the sport. He has won numerous awards, set countless records, and is known for his incredible athleticism, skill, and determination on the field.

  • Full Name: Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro
  • Born: February 5, 1985
  • Birthplace: Funchal, Madeira, Portugal
  • Profession: Professional Footballer
  • Teams Played For: Sporting CP, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, Al Nassr
  • Nationality: Portuguese

Career Highlights

  1. 2003: Signed by Manchester United
  2. 2009: Moved to Real Madrid for a then-world record fee
  3. 2016: Helped Portugal win the UEFA European Championship
  4. 2018: Joined Juventus
  5. 2021: Returned to Manchester United
  6. 2023: Signed with Al Nassr
